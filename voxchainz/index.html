THE FIRST CUSTOM NFT JEWELRY LINE

SOLANA'S BIGGEST FLEX

VOXCHAINZ is the first bespoke, luxury jewelry line of the NFT space. Our genesis collection, built on Solana, will consist of 999 custom voxel gold chain necklaces made from your favorite pixel-art NFTs that you already own & cherish.

Turn your favorite Solana pixel art NFT, such as an SMB, Thugbird or Boogle, into 'wearable' digital jewelry. As long as you own the pixel art NFT, you can turn it into a VOXCHAIN. (Resolution limit 100x100px)

How does it work?

VOX GOLD TICKETS

A VOX Gold Ticket allows you to commission 1 gold chain. They can be acquired via public mint for 5 SOL or secondary marketplace. To redeem your chain fill out this form: VOXCHAIN REDEMPTION FORM

Each chain can be customized to your liking in a variety of ways (more info below) and within 1 week turnaround your gold ticket will dynamically update in-wallet to your rendered chain.

Along the way, we’ll work with you individually via Discord to make sure your exact specifications are met. You’ll be able to approve previews before the final render is updated in-wallet, or you can opt to let us take full creative control, skipping the approvals process.

CUSTOMIZATION

VOXCHAINZ are customizable in a variety of ways. Each color within your pixel art can be assigned a texture / material. There are 3 MATERIALS to choose from: METALLIC, which has a shiny, glimmering sharp contrast; JEWEL, which has a semi-transparent glassy quality; and GLOW, which emits neon light.

For instance, with an SMB you can choose to make your background color JEWEL, the face METAL & its eyes GLOW.

You can substitute colors, and even add pixel art graphics to the background or face (maybe a tattoo for your ThugBird?). Does your PFP have a custom pixel art background? Provide that file for us and we can add it to your VOXCHAIN!

RARITY & UPGRADES

GOLD & PLATINUM

As all of our NFTs are custom 1/1s we don't have a standard rarity system. We simply have two tiers, GOLD (rare) & PLATINUM (ultra-rare). In order to get a platinum VOXCHAIN you must acquire a VOX PLATINUM TICKET via our Public Mint or Secondary Marketplace.

Alternatively you can use 2 VOX GOLD TICKETS to redeem 1 platinum chain, or you can use 1 VOX GOLD TICKET to upgrade your current gold chain to a platinum chain.

MELTING

CHANGE A CHAIN

Another flagship feature we offer is "melting" of your VOXCHAINZ i.e. re-customizing / changing the artwork on the face of the chain to a new pixel art NFT you own. In order to do so, you must combine 2 VOXCHAINZ to melt-down into 1 fresh gold ticket, burning the total supply by 1.

We feel that this helps to make the chains a little more liquid on secondary marketplace and it also serves as a deflationary mechanism, making the collection more and more scarce each time someone chooses to re-customize.
